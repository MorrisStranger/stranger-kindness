Our Story

Because no one should be a stranger to a warm meal

Every night, restaurants and cafes discard tonnes of perfectly good food. At the same time, thousands of people go to sleep hungry on our streets.

No Stranger To Kindness was founded to bridge that gap. We partner with local eateries to rescue surplus food before it goes to waste and deliver it — with care and dignity — to shelters, outreach centres, and directly to people sleeping rough.

Our name carries a simple truth: kindness shouldn't be reserved for people we know. Every stranger deserves compassion, and every meal shared is a reminder that we're all connected.

The Process

How It Works

From kitchen to community in three simple steps

01

Restaurants Alert Us

At the end of service, partner restaurants and cafes log their surplus meals through our app. We know exactly what's available and when.

02

Volunteers Collect

Our network of dedicated volunteers picks up the food in insulated bags, ensuring it stays fresh and safe during transport.

03

Meals Are Shared

Food reaches shelters, outreach vans, and street teams within the hour. No one eats alone — every delivery comes with a friendly face.
